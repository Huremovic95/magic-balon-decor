About Us:


Magic Balon Decor is a company founded in 2019. It deal with the sale of all kinds of balloons and decoration of spaces for all kinds of events. We are made up of a small team of creative people who have combined their vast experience, talent, creativity and positive energy to make every important moment in your life fun and unforgettable. Why Magic Balon Decor? Because our company will help you organize the special occasion of your dreams. Our mission is to make each of your events magical. In addition to the glamorous decoration full of zircons, crystals and various details, we also offer phase format printing, welcome cards, thank you cards, invitations, banners and large format printings.
